Room Reservation

This is the initial step where guests inquire about room availability and make reservations. This can be done through various channels such as the hotel's website, mobile apps, phone calls, or through third-party booking platforms.

Booking Confirmation

Once the reservation is made, the hotel sends a confirmation to the guest, either via email or SMS, confirming the details of their booking including dates, room type, and any special requests.

Check-In Process

Upon arrival, guests check in at the hotel's reception desk. Here, they provide identification, payment information, and any specific preferences they may have regarding their stay.

Room Assignment

The hotel assigns a room to the guest based on their preferences and availability. Key cards or physical keys are provided to access the room.

Billing and Payment

Throughout the guest's stay, any additional services availed (such as room service, minibar, etc.) are recorded electronically. Upon check-out, the total bill is generated.

Check-Out Process

Guests return their room keys, settle their bills, and provide feedback on their stay if desired. The hotel may also offer express check-out options where guests can settle their bills in advance to expedite the departure process.

Invoicing and Receipts

After payment is made, the hotel provides guests with an itemized receipt detailing the charges incurred during their stay. For business travelers, this receipt is often necessary for expense reimbursement purposes.

Post-Stay Communication

Some hotels follow up with guests after their stay to gather feedback and ensure satisfaction. This communication can help hotels improve their services and maintain guest loyalty.
